About

I hadn't planned on making programming, or web design specifically, part of my career path. I have my BA in Comparative Literature from the University of California at Riverside, extra coursework in Mathematics and Physics, and a work history ranging from private tutoring to medical office administration to lighting and sound for live theatre.

Then one day, my brother asked me (not for the first time): "So, when are you going to learn to code?"

I didn't have an answer at the time. The more I thought about it, though, the better an idea it turned out to be. So here I am.
